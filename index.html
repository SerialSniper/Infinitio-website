Infinitio

What's Infinitio?

We are making an ultra realistic pack too! Ours, differently from others,
will be more focused on a brutalist style, and it'll contain rougher textures.

What makes it different

What makes our pack different from others is that we'll go for full quality:
every single texture will be highly worked on and will be optimized for the best experience.

Here are some examples:

  • Every texture will have a normal and spectral map, as it's the standard for such packs
  • Most of the textures, those that fit well, will have ctm! It indeed will transform into other materials between blocks
  • Every texture will be almost perfectly seamless! This means that you won't have any ugly lines between them
  • Many textures will be wider than 1x1 block: some will take up 4x4 blocks and many will be randomized too, to avoid repetitions
